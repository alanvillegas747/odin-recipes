Rich and Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Description

This tomato basil soup uses real butter, fresh basil, and heavy cream, which are the secrets to the richness of this soup.

Additionally, it's rich with condiments that enrich the soup's flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium ripe tomatoes - peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 4 cups tomato juice
  • 14 leaves fresh basil
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients!
  2. Place tomatoes and juice in a stockpot over medium heat; bring to a simmer.
  3. Cook until tomatoes have softened, about 30 minutes
  4. Remove the pot from heat, then add basil leaves to the pot.
  5. Use an immersion blender to puree soup until smooth.
  6. Stir in heavy cream and butter. Cook and stir over medium heat until butter is melted in about 5 minutes. Do not boil. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
